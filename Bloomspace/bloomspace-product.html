About Anjamani Pink

The Aglaonema Anjamani is amongst the easier going plant that wins the heart of many plant fashionista. Matched with light pink Serres pot, it further accentuates its eye catching leaves with pink blotches on a green base.

This plant is extremely tolerant under both moist and dry conditions, and they can live with minimum light, making it the perfect choice as gift.

Care Instructions

Light

Prefers bright indirect sunlight. Avoid direct sun exposure.

Watering

Keep soil moist but not soggy. Water when top inch is dry.

Temperature

Ideal temperature between 18-24°C. Avoid cold drafts.

Fertilizer

Feed monthly during growing season with balanced fertilizer.
